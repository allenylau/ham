About

We’re Monina and Allen, a two-person design team based in Chicago. Good design works, it’s made with care, and it lasts. We’ve worked at the New York Times, Block, FiftyThree, and WeTransfer, and partnered with plenty of people just getting started. This is us just getting started too, and we’d love to build something with you.

We’re into

Film on the big screen. Dim sum. Farmers markets and fresh flowers. Cozy neighborhood cafes. Public libraries. Nature centers. Henry. Home-cooked meals. A really good nap. Filipino bakeries. Ballpoint pens worth writing with.

How we can help

Branding

We build the visual world around your business. Logo, colors, typography, photography style, and how everything moves and feels. The things that make your brand recognizable and consistent wherever it shows up.

Marketing Assets

Posters, flyers, social graphics, digital ads. This is the stuff that lives in the world, on walls, in feeds, in inboxes. It deserves as much thought as anything else we make.

Websites

A custom website that reflects who you are. We can start from scratch or work with a brand you already have and translate it into something that works online.

Presentation &
Pitch Decks

The thing that gets you in the room and keeps people’s attention once you’re there. We help with the structure, the story, and the design.

Illustration

From icons and spot illustrations to editorial concepts, characters, and storyboarding. We illustrate across a wide range of styles.
